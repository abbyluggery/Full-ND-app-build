## Instructions for Creating Records

### Step 1: Create Job_Posting__c Records
1. Go to **Job Postings** tab (or App Launcher > Job Postings)
2. Click **New**
3. Copy/paste fields from each job above
4. **Important**: Leave analysis fields blank - they will auto-populate when you click "Analyze with AI"
5. Create all 10 records

### Step 2: Create Daily_Routine__c Records
1. Go to **Daily Routines** tab (or App Launcher > Daily Routines)
2. Click **New**
3. Copy/paste fields from each day above
4. Create all 10 records
5. These show energy variation over time for dashboard visualizations

### Step 3: Create Master_Resume_Config__c Record (CRITICAL!)
1. Go to **App Launcher** > Search "Master Resume Config"
2. Click **New**
3. **IMPORTANT**: Replace placeholder text with YOUR actual information:
   - Your real name, email, phone
   - Your actual work experience
   - Your real education
   - Your certifications
   - Customize skills based on your background
4. This record is used by Resume Generator - must be accurate!

### Step 4: Create Resume_Package__c Records (AFTER Master Resume Config exists)
1. Go to **Resume Packages** tab
2. Click **New**
3. Select Job_Posting__c from lookup
4. You can either:
   - Let Resume Generator auto-populate Resume_Text__c and Cover_Letter_Text__c
   - OR manually fill in for these samples
5. Create 2-3 records
